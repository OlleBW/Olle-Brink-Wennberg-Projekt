Beginners guide to Fate


Hello! This is going to be a short and easy guide to the Fate universe. I am going to try my best at explaining and guiding you to the easiest entry point into this beheamot of a franchise. If you've ever heard of the Fate franchise, you know just how in depth and confusing it is. I know of many who have avoided the show because of it's complexity. I will not be going into all of Fate, but instead only cover the most important timelines. On this front page I will be giving the short answer as to how to watch the series but as you will find out if you keep reading, the short answer is too short.

The Short Answer

The first series you wanna watch is Fate Stay/Night and that is where you should start, but as you will soon find out that is in itself complicated. After you have gotten through Stay/Night you can pretty much watch anything in any order and it doesn't matter. That was the short answer. To continue the guide, make your way Here.
